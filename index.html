So, Who is 'Pat' then?

I'm a London, Ontario based, content and communications writer that has been called a 'swiss army knife' when it comes to marketing. Over my 10+ years of marketing experience I've managed multiple social media accounts, created countless brand voice and tone guides, executed high-profile nation-wide campaigns, introduced procedures and tools that reduced project turnaround time, I've guided co-workers through upgrading their career through training and development. I've lead teams, launched products, managed events, hosted podcasts, been multiple mascots, driven change, and continually learned.

Since marketing is such a diverse skillset, I've been able to lend my skills to various industries; from office furniture to video games. This variety has allowed me to sharpen my skillset and pushed me to develop my capabilities outside of simply copywriting and lean into digital media as well.

If I'm not analyzing a company's online presence, you'll normally find me enjoying long walks with a podcast (especially if it's hockey related), spending time with my partner and daughter, or concocting a new recipe in the kitchen to try and out-do my strangely-famous and fan-favourite Dill Pickle Steaks.
